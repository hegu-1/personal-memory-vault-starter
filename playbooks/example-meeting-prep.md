# Preparing for a meeting

## Purpose

For any meeting longer than 30 minutes, run this checklist.

## Steps

### 24 hours before

1. Define the **meeting outcome**: what 1–3 results do you want when it's over?
2. List the **agenda** (3–5 bullets) and send it to all participants
3. Gather **the data / materials needed** and share them ahead of time
4. **Anticipate objections** — at least one, and prepare a response

### 30 minutes before

1. Reread the agenda, verify it aligns with the outcome
2. Prepare a 30-second opener: "Our goal today is X, the agenda is 1 / 2 / 3"
3. Prepare a closing question: "Did we achieve X today? What's the next step?"

### During the meeting

- Stick to the agenda; no tangent runs longer than 5 minutes
- Confirm key decisions explicitly: "We all agree on X, right?"
- Record action items + owner + deadline

### Within 24 hours after

- Send a meeting summary (outcome + decisions + action items) to participants
- Follow up on at least one of your own action items

## Anti-patterns / don't

- ❌ Meeting with no agenda
- ❌ Sending the agenda 5 minutes before
- ❌ Nobody recording action items
- ❌ No summary afterward
